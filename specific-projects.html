SPECIFIC PROJECTS & SERVICES

As clients' specific legal needs and concerns are in a constant state of flux, J.A. Reyes Law Office also provides the following legal services in accordance with the clients' unique or specific requirements:

  1. Legal Consultancy/Legal Retainer — The Law Office provides clients with legal consultancy services for an agreed period of time to assist the clients in their various legal needs such as but not limited to, drafting and review of contracts, issuance of legal opinions, representations in official meetings especially with third parties, acting as general corporate counsel and giving legal advice with respect to certain aspects of their businesses and ventures.
  2. Preparation and Issuance of Legal Opinions on Various Legal Topics
  3. Construction and Evaluation of Contracts, Agreements and other Legal Documents and Instruments
  4. Drafting and/or Review of Specialized Contracts and other Agreements
  5. Business Establishment Assistance — The Law Office provides assistance in business incorporation, filing and processing of the necessary applications for BIR registration, local government business permits, as well as filing and processing of registration applications with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI), Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and other government agencies.
  6. Legal Assistance in Property Exchanges and Titling of Real Properties
  7. Assistance before the Philippine Senate and the House of Representatives and Formulating or Drafting of Bills and Ordinances for Public Officials
