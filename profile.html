LAWYER'S PROFILE

Atty. Jen Reyes graduated First Honorable Mention in Law School and was a consistent Dean's Lister and Honor Roll member all throughout her academic life. She has always been devoted to aiming for eminence in everything she does.

Mentored by the greats in the legal profession and with ardent inclination for excellence, Atty. Jen Reyes established and founded her own Law Office, devout in making certain that justice is done.

She is also a Law Professor, teaching Civil Law Review 1, Commercial Law Review, Land Titles and Deeds and Legal Forms.

Ergo, the solid academic background, compassionate method and superb communication skills the Law Firm provide make the clients optimistic about the result of their legal concerns.
