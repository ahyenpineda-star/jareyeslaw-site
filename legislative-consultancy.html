LEGISLATIVE CONSULTANCY

J.A. Reyes Law Office also assists public servants in formulating or drafting bills, ordinances, regulations and local laws.

It also provides assistance before the Philippine Senate and the House of Representatives.

Furthermore, it imparts legal advice to public officers and public servants about legal issues that affect their office, duties, projects and programs and public servants also seek its assistance about compliance with and legality of laws and regulations.

The legal services include educating public servants about relevant laws, including:

  • Administrative Law
  • Election Law
  • Law on Public Officers
  • The Local Government Code

The Law Office also reviews contracts and counsels candidates in their election campaigns and compliance with election laws.
