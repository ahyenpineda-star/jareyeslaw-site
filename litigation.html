LITIGATION

J.A. Reyes Law Office is committed to providing clients with unequivocal and driven legal representation and support.

The Law Office offers comprehensive advice and assistance to its clients and abets them in formulating and accomplishing procedures and solutions for complicated areas of litigation before the courts and other administrative bodies and agencies.

It acts for its clients in civil, criminal and administrative cases before trial courts, quasi-judicial agencies and appellate courts, enforcing its clients' legal rights from investigation, arbitration, to the conscientious process of gathering evidence, filing of the necessary pleadings and pre-trial, trial, and appeal stages.
